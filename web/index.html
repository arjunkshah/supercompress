Questions & Answers

What does one API call actually do?

POST /v1/agent/turn runs Tavily search, gathers your Composio apps, merges your context_blocks, compresses memory, and answers via Nebius — in order, every time.

Do I need my own Tavily or Nebius keys?

No. Sponsor keys are hosted on our API. You only connect Composio in the dashboard for per-user app access.

How do I integrate in my app?

Pass your user's message as query and serialize tasks, reminders, or state as context_blocks. Show answer in your chat UI.

Can I use just compression?

Yes — POST /v1/compress/blocks is available for advanced use. The main product is the full agent turn.
